Sensei Viktoriya

Head Coach

Sensei Viktoriya Brezgin has over 35 years of experience in judo and sambo. She always puts her students' best interests first and teaches hard work, discipline, and confidence that can be used not only on the mat but off the mat too. Sensei Viktoriya is the head competitive judo coach for the cadet and junior team, where she has brought athletes to national and international levels.

  • Over 25 years of coaching experience.
  • Former member of the Ukrainian Judo and Sambo National Team.
  • World Cup Judo medalist.
  • 3× Sambo World Championships medalist.
  • World Cup team champion in Sambo.
  • NCCP Level 2 Certified.
  • 2nd Dan Black Belt in Judo and Black Belt in Taekwondo.
